The Ocean, one of the main balances of our environment.

Covering more than 70% of the Earth's surface, the ocean ranks as the largest ecosystem on our planet.

On that date, the whole world is informed about the importance of preserving and caring for our oceans, as well as the negative impact that humans have on them.

How can I help?

The best way to help is by conserving, and avoiding throwing garbage in inappropriate places.
Avoiding water waste is extremely important, only 3% of all water on the planet is fresh water.

The importance of the oceans

Despite all its importance for the environment and the world economy, the oceans are being degraded. Currently, there are studies that show that approximately 13 tons of plastic reach the ocean every year. This plastic is often ingested by marine fauna, such as fish and turtles, and other animals such as birds that feed on fish, causing the death of 100,000 marine animals a year, in addition to other damage.

Projects

Faced with this alarming situation, the UN declared the period between 2021 and 2030 as the Ocean Decade for Sustainable Development.
The goal is to mobilize scientists, politicians, companies and civil society for research and innovation.
